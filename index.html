Concept

During Japan’s economic bubble of the 1980s, condominium construction increased dramatically...

Today, a 40-year-old condominium is often perceived as “old.” However, as the stock continues to grow...

Rather than adopting a complete skeleton-and-infill approach, this renovation employs an upcycling strategy...

By reinterpreting the given rather than replacing it, the project proposes a renewed architectural lifespan.
